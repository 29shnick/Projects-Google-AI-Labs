# AI Prompt Engineering Case Study: Pet Modeling Campaign

## Project Objective
To explore AI-assisted creative direction and prompt engineering to develop a professional, modern, and playful social media campaign aimed at securing talent agency gigs for a French Bulldog.

## Phase 1: Ideation & Conceptualization
We began by establishing the core subject, mood, and primary objective to generate tailored creative concepts.

**Initial Concept Prompts:**
* "I need some ideas for a [social media post] about [my french bulldog]."
* "The style should be modern, and the mood should be calm."
* "Please give me 3 different creative concepts. For each concept, provide a: Description: A brief explanation of the visual layout. Color Palette: A set of colors that fit the mood."
* "For context, [ability photo modeling]. The tone of the writing should be playful in order to [primary objective, to get gigs]"

## Phase 2: Copywriting
We generated social media text options that fit the modern and calm aesthetic, ready to be paired with the visuals.

**Copywriting Prompt:**
* "Generate 3 options for text for a social media post about french bulldog breed."

## Phase 3: Image Generation & Iterative Refining
Using the concepts developed in Phase 1, we generated an initial image and used iterative prompting to refine the layout, lighting, and artistic style.

**Base Image Generation Prompt:**
* "Based on the [chosen creative concept] and the text '[insert your favorite headline/text here]', generate a detailed image. The image should feature [subject/main focus] in a [visual style, e.g. photographic, 3D render, or oil painting] style. Ensure the composition includes [layout requirements, e.g. plenty of empty space at the top or a blurred background]. The overall mood should be [mood/vibe]."

**Refining Prompts (The Iteration Process):**
1. **Layout Adjustment:** "This is great, but can you move the [dog] to the [left side] to leave more room for my text on the [right]?"
2. **Lighting & Color:** "Can you make the overall lighting brighter and more natural and change the background colors to [shades of blue and silver]"
3. **Detail Correction:** "Please keep the same style, but add subtle texture and remove one of the flowers."
4. **Final Style Transfer:** "I like the layout, but can you change the style from [current style] to a [minimalist vector illustration]?"
